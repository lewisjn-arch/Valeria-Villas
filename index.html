About

VALERIA VILLAS

Discover luxury living in our exclusive estate of 37 elegant 4-bedroom mansionettes, each thoughtfully designed with three spacious lounges—including a stunning double-volume formal lounge, a cozy family room, and a stylish rooftop lounge complete with a pergola.

Enjoy the perfect blend of comfort and sophistication with private gardens, dedicated DSQ, and secure parking for two. Elevate your lifestyle in a community crafted for those who desire both elegance and functionality.

Located just 6 kilometers from Thika superhighway, along the Eastern Bypass, you have access to many recreational, educational and medical facilities within Kamakis area and Ruiru municipality.

Community Amenities

The estate is surrounded by a range of recreational facilities that make leisure time both enjoyable and convenient. The nearby Thika Road and its environs also offer entertainment options such as Ruiru Sports Club, Vipe Fun Park, Leisure Park, Pro Kidz Amusement, Wadau Resort, Mashinani Waterfall and Afro Sayari for those looking for an active lifestyle or a fun outing. Nestled in a prime location in Ruiru, the estate is near an array of top-notch dining and hospitality options. Whether you're looking for a quick bite or a fine dining experience, the surrounding area has diverse restaurants offering local delicacies such as Java, Art Cafe, The OX Sportsbar, Greenspot Gardens, The Roast, The Mark Hotel and many more. View them

Conveniently located near Thika Superhighway, the estate offers unparalleled access to some of the region’s premiere shopping destinations. Malls like Spur Mall, Garden City Mall, The Nord Mall, Uni-city mall, Mountain mall, Quickmart and a Naivas branch along Eastern Bypass also feature entertainment facilities, food courts and children’s play zones. View them

Conveniently located just 100 metres from Eastern Bypass and 6 kilometers from Thika superhighway at Ruiru round-about, the estate offers ease of access for those commuting to work and those driving to and from work. The estate is also easily accessible by school transportation. View them

The location of the estate offers ease of access to medical facilities less than 5 kilometers away. This offers perfect access in case of any medical emergencies. The hospitals in the area include; Agha Khan University Hospital, RFH Hospital, Kenyatta University Teaching & Referral Hospital, BSR Hospital Nairobi and well-known pharmaceutical outlets like Goodlife Pharmacy, all along Eastern Bypass. WHY VALERIA VILLAS?

Valeria Villas provides a luxurious and convenient lifestyle.

Each maisonette offers 240 square metres of spacious and elegantly designed living areas, perfect for families seeking comfort and style. With 4 bedrooms and 3 lounges, there's ample space for relaxation and entertainment, ensuring every member of the household has their own private retreat.

Built with precision and attention to detail, our maisonettes showcase high-quality craftsmanship and durable materials. Every aspect of the construction, from the foundation to the unique finishes, is designed with you in mind and to withstand the test of time.

Located 6 kilometres from Thika Superhighway and 100 metres off Eastern Bypass, the estate offers convenience and accessibility to key amenities such as schools, shopping centers, restaurants, and recreational facilities. Whether clients are commuting to work or exploring the local area, everything they need is just a stone-throw away.

The estate provides a secure and private environment for residents, with gated access manned by a security company and surveillance systems to ensure peace of mind at all times. There is ample solar street-lighting at night to ensure more security and safety. Families can relax knowing that their safety and privacy are top priorities within the community.

Depending on the stage of construction, buyers may have the opportunity to customize certain features of their maisonette, allowing them to personalize their living space and create a home that truly reflects their unique style and preferences. This option is offered within our terms and conditions.

For clients who value privacy and independence, each unit offers the added convenience of a detached servants' quarters. This separate living space provides accommodation for household staff or guests, ensuring that residents can enjoy their main residence without compromise.
