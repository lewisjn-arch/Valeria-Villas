About VALERIA VILLAS

Discover luxury living in our exclusive estate of 37 elegant 4-bedroom mansionettes, each thoughtfully designed with three spacious lounges—including a stunning double-volume formal lounge, a cozy family room, and a stylish rooftop lounge complete with a pergola.

Enjoy the perfect blend of comfort and sophistication with private gardens, dedicated DSQ, and secure parking for two. Elevate your lifestyle in a community crafted for those who desire both elegance and functionality.

Located just 6 kilometers from Thika superhighway, along the Eastern by-pass, you have access to many recreational, educational and medical facilities within Kamakis area and Ruiru municipality.

Community Amenities

Nestled in a prime location in Ruiru, the estate is near an array of top-notch dining and hospitality options. Whether you're looking for a quick bite or a fine dining experience, the surrounding area has diverse restaurants offering local delicacies, while being close to Greenspot gardens, famous for its roast meat.

Conveniently located near Thika Superhighway, the estate offers unparalleled access to some of the region’s premiere shopping destinations. These malls also feature entertainment facilities, food courts and children’s play zones, making them ideal for both shopping trips and family outings.

The estate's location offers easy access to a variety of reputable schools and universities. Nearby, you'll find Mountain View School and Kasarani Group of Schools. For higher education, the estate is within reach of top-tier universities such as JKUAT (Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology) and Kenyatta University.

The estate is surrounded by a range of recreational facilities that make leisure time both enjoyable and convenient. The nearby Thika Road and its environs also offer entertainment options such as cinemas, bowling alleys, and golf courses for those looking for an active lifestyle or a fun outing.

Due to being located in a highly growing area along eastern by-pass, the location of the estate offers ease of access to medical facilities like RFH hospital which is just 2 kilometers away. This offers perfect access in case of any medical emergencies.

Conveniently located just 100 metres from Eastern by-pass and 6 kilometers from Thika superhighway at Ruiru round-about, the estate offers ease of access for both commuting to work and driving to and from work. The estate also easily accessible for school transportation for children living in the estate.

WHY VALERIA VILLAS?

Each maisonette offers spacious and elegantly designed living areas, perfect for families or individuals seeking comfort and style. With 4 bedrooms and 3 lounges, there's ample space for relaxation and entertainment, ensuring every member of the household has their own private retreat.

Built with precision and attention to detail, these maisonettes showcase high-quality craftsmanship and premium materials. From the foundation to the finishing touches, every aspect of the construction is designed to withstand the test of time.

Situated in a desirable location, the estate offers convenience and accessibility to key amenities such as schools, shopping centers, restaurants, and recreational facilities. Whether clients are commuting to work or exploring the local area, everything they need is just a stone-throw away.

The estate provides a secure and private environment for residents, with gated access and surveillance systems ensuring peace of mind at all times. Families can relax knowing that their safety and privacy are top priorities within the community.

Depending on the stage of construction, buyers may have the opportunity to customize certain features of their maisonette, allowing them to personalize their living space and create a home that truly reflects their unique style and preferences.

For clients who value privacy and independence, each unit offers the added convenience of a detached servants' quarters. This separate living space provides accommodation for household staff or guests, ensuring that residents can enjoy their main residence without compromise.
